Animal Care

General Housekeeping

All dog and cat food is kept in the pantry in the kitchen. Medicines and supplements are kept on the counter next to the sink.

Extra cat litter, dog peepads and cleaning supplies are kept next to the litter tray in the dining room. Emergency contacts list is kept on the fridge.

Feeding Instructions

Both dogs and cats get 2 tablespoons of corresponding wet food and 1/2 cup of corresponding dry food. They are all fed twice a day (Once in AM and once in PM).

Both dogs get a cranberry bite supplement at breakfast. Everybody gets 1 squirt of salmon oil at breakfast.

Medicine Routine

Millie receives 1 marked capsule sprinkled over her wet food for breakfast.

Max gets 1 marked antibiotic tablet with his breakfast (Hide this within his dry food). Both cats get marked liquid medicines at dinner (Use the accompanying syringes to measure 1ml and add this to their wet food).

Walk Schedule

The dogs typically get walked 3 times per day. Once in the morning before we leave for work, once in the afternoon when we get home from work and again before bedtime.

The first and second walks are typically longer and they can be taking the length of the street down to the last mulch area and back. The last walk before bedtime is usually just a quick one and they can be taken to the mulch area opposite the house.

Bedtime Arrangements

Everyone is seperated at bedtime as we have had a few issues between them in the past.

  • Millie - Sleeps in the basement with the door closed
  • Jerry - Stays in the living room
  • Jasmine - Sleeps in her crate in the master bedroom
  • Max - Sleeps on the bed in the master bedroom
  • Night lights are set to automatically come on and music is left on low.

Millie sleeps in the basement with the door closed. We also leave the Alexa Echo playing music for her. Jerry is given the run of the rest of the house.
